menu: principal
-> Welcome to nfcdbi principal menu, what to do:
> db explorer   (list items in the db)
> item info     (print database info of the next tag)
> backpack      (next tag(s) are item(s) that you are going to use)
> charge        (next tag(s) are battery(ies) that you are going to charge)
> register      (if the next tag is not in the databse, it will be inserted)
